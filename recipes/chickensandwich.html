Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Description

A quick-on-the-go chicken sandwich that can be prepped as a work lunch, and reheatable and customized to your choice of toppings and meats

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • tomato paste
  • spinach
  • salt
  • pepper
  • hot sauce
  • bread
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Get 2 slices of bread and place them on a plate
  2. Put a slice of cheese on one of the pieces of bread
  3. Put a serving of tomato paste and spinach on the same bread
  4. Retrieve cooking pan and place on stove top
  5. Prep cooking pan with cooking spray and preheat at level 7
  6. Cut 112g of chicken breast as flat pieces and cook it using a pan stovetop for 4 minutes on both sides
  7. After chicken is cooked, place chicken on the bread with toppings
  8. Season chicken and toppings with salt & pepper and hot sauce
  9. Cover chicken & toppings on top of bread with the other piece of bread
  10. Wait for 5 minutes and enjoy!
