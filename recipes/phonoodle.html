Pho Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

A traditional style, simplified version of Vietnamese signature noodle dish. It is customizable by substituting meat and choice of toppings

Ingredients

  • cilantro
  • meat (chicken/beef)
  • viet style pho noodles
  • jalopeno
  • spring onions
  • yellow onions
  • chicken pho flavor paste

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water proportionate to amount of noodles to be used
  2. Bring pot to boil
  3. Add to the pot dry Viet style pho noodles for 7-8 minutes
  4. Drain noodles in coriander
  5. Put noodles in serving bowl and add sliced jalopeno, and diced yellow onions and green onions
  6. Fill new pot with water proportionate to meat and amount of pho flavor paste
  7. Add proportional amount of pho paste and meat to the new pot of water
  8. Bring pot to boil and wait 3 minutes
  9. Pour contents of the pot into the serving bowl and wait 15 minutes to cool down
  10. Add lemon and basil and sauces or bean sprouts as needed
  11. Enjoy your helping of pho!
